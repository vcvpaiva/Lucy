**Adjoint functors**
A pair of functors $F : \mathcal{C} \to \mathcal{D}$ and $G : \mathcal{D} \to \mathcal{C}$ are adjoint if there is a natural isomorphism 
$$
Hom_{\mathcal{D}}(F(X),Y) \cong Hom_{\mathcal{C}}(X,G(Y)) 
$$

Mathematical area: Category theory
